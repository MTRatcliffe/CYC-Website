An important 2026 update from Cambodia Youth For Change

After many years of contributing to positive change in the local area, Cambodia Youth For Change is now entering its final chapter, winding down the school and working closely with other charities in the area to help ensure a smooth transition.

We are deeply grateful to all donors, volunteers, staff, partners and supporters who have contributed to this journey and helped make a lasting difference in the lives of young people.
